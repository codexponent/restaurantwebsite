Here at The Monsieur Benjamin we would be happy to host your private party. We have a wide variety of ways to use our various rooms, bars and dining areas to make your event a memorable one for you and your guests. From business and formal meetings to the most intimate of gatherings, our beautifully restored spaces and delicious food are the ingredients necessary to make your event a successful one.
Please send requests for information to
PrivateEvents@TheGreyRestaurant.com or call us at 912-662-5999, Ext 0.



The Shower Room


Max Capacity: 20 People


Elegance is the operative term to describe the Shower Room. With a large communal table capable of seating 14, this room can be supplemented with an additional table to accommodate up to 20. Your guests can relax in a separate seating area and a window overlooking our main dining room connects you to the energy that makes The Grey a unique venue in Savannah.



The Boiler Room


Max Capacity: 10 People


An intimate setting for up to 10 people, The Boiler Room is the perfect place to host a small gathering for business, friends or family. Just off of our wine room, this is the most private space in The Grey. With its exposed brick, elegant lighting and old world charm, you and your guests will feel warm and welcome. And, there is a piano and a guitar at your disposal… just in case.



The Diner Bar


Max Capacity: 20 People


With its Hopper-esque picture window overlooking MLK, the Diner Bar is transportive in its appearance, personality and focus on craft cocktails. With the ability to host up to 45 people, this is an ideal gathering spot for a casual business event or drinks and snacks with family and friends in a comfortable environment. We are happy to host all types of events in the Diner Bar including happy hours, meet ups and wedding-related parties.



The Yard


Max Capacity: 40 People


The sky is the limit in The Yard. With communal seating for up to 150, we can also customize your experience through our relationships with our vendors to provide as much seating as you need, tents, outdoor cooling and entertainment. The Yard is a party waiting to happen.
